BASE SIEGE v29.6 — Merge Cannons & Hero Balance Update
🔫 new: blaster cannon
🔫 New 2×2 merge tower! Surround/build 4 level-1 BLASTER towers in a square, then hit "MERGE 2x2 INTO BLASTER CANNON" to fuse them
🔫 Path A — GATLING FORTRESS: one big central minigun plus 4 smaller corner guns, all firing in 5-barrel bursts with no charge-up, for relentless sustained DPS
💥 Path B — SHOCK CANNON: charges up like a railgun, then unleashes one massive circular blast that damages AND stuns every enemy caught in it
⚖ balance
⚡ RAILGUN CANNON (2×2 merge) significantly buffed across every tier — its DPS was barely ahead of a single-tile RAILGUN despite costing 4 towers and 4 tiles; damage now scales to properly reward the merge
🏆 HERO: SCORE MACHINE nerfed — combat buff % (DMG/speed/range) reduced ~35% and score-multiplier bonus reduced ~45% across every upgrade tier, both paths. It was providing far too much power for a single hero slot
🎨 vfx
🎨 Fixed a bug where the 2×2 merge cannons fired from an off-center point instead of their true middle — the BLASTER CANNON in particular looked like only one gun was ever shooting
🎨 GATLING FORTRESS now fires visibly from all 5 barrels (4 corners + center), each with its own muzzle flash and sparks, instead of every shot spawning from one shared point
🎨 SHOCK CANNON charge-up now has a brighter windup glow, crackling arcs, and a bigger dual-ring blast + screen shake on detonation
